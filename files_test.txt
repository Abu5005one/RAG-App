Kerala is a state in the southern part of India known for its palm-lined beaches, backwaters, and eco-tourism.
Famous places include Munnar, Alleppey, Thekkady, Wayanad, and Kochi. Kerala is also known for Ayurveda, Kathakali dance, and houseboat cruises.
The best time to visit is from October to March. Traditional foods include Sadya, Appam, and Kerala fish curry.
